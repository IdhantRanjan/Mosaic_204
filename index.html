About Mosaic

Built by students,
for students.

Mosaic is the district-wide club discovery platform for IPSD 204 — designed to make it easy for every student to find where they belong.

Our Mission

We believe every student deserves to find their place.

High school is full of opportunities — clubs, activities, competitions, creative outlets, and communities waiting to be discovered. But too often, students don't know where to look or how to connect with what they love. Mosaic changes that.

Our platform makes club discovery personal, simple, and district-wide. Whether you're passionate about robotics, want to join a service organization, or just want to try something new — Mosaic helps you find it in minutes.
